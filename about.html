Details of The look App

Home Page

This section will display the general products,services that are offered by the salon plus the average rating of each stylist at the salon.

Booking Page

In this page clients will be able to make appointments with the salon on specific days and time of their convenience.

Products Page

In this page clients will be able to view the available products at the salon and also place their orders which will then be added to the cart and display the total amount that they will be charged at the salon.

Notification Page

On this page clients will be able to view the avaliable discounts offered by the salon and also it will be used to display adverts by the salon.

SideBar

This section will show the users profile, display the discount percentage gained, their favorite stylist and the logout button that will enable users to signout of the app.

Users Bookings

This page will display all the bookings by the user. They will also be able to see the time for each service and their respective cost.

Stylists

This section will show the respective stylist chosen by the user and they will be able to make appointments with them. Users will also be able to see the costs for the services offered.

Product Description

This page will display the respective product selected by the user and its description. This will help users know more about what they are buying.

Signup Page

This section will enable new users to create accounts to be able to use and access the services offered by the Look App.

Top

Login Page

In this section, Users who have already created accounts will be able to signin to the App and access the products and services.
